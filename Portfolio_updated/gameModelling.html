Hidden Object Game 3D Modelling

Overview

This project focuses on creating a detailed 3D scene based on tabletop games or toys from popular culture.

Objective

The primary objective of this project is to demonstrate competence in 3D Computer Graphics.

Key Requirements

  • Modelling: Create at least 5 unique objects from scratch.
  • Texturing: Incorporate a transmissive object, a reflective surface, bump mapping and texture mapping.
  • Lighting & Rendering: Create a photorealistic or stylistic render.
  • Environment: Create either an indoor or outdoor scene.
  • Render the final scene at 4K resolution (3840x2160 pixels).

Outcome

Available by January 25, 2025
