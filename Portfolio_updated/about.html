Get to know more About me

Hi, It's Nithya!

Born and raised in Milan, I've always been passionate about technology, which led me to pursue a Bachelor Degree in Computer Systems and Networks Security. During my final exam, I discovered my love for web development and its potential impact on people’s lives.

Now, I'm in Dublin pursuing a Master in Interactive Digital Media at Trinity College, where I’m focusing on mastering web development and creating web applications that not only function seamlessly but also represent people and the stories behind them. I’m particularly excited about exploring how user-centered design principles can enhance digital experiences, allowing me to craft engaging interfaces that resonate with people.

When I’m not immersed in web design and coding, you can find me watching action movies, reading a good book, or working out to stay energised and inspired.
